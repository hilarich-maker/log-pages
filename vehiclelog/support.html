VehicleLog Organizer Support

VehicleLog Organizer helps you keep vehicles, maintenance records, documents, attachments, and reminders organized on your device. This page answers common questions and tells you how to reach support.

Local-first and offline-first. VehicleLog Organizer stores your data on your device. It does not require an account, and the current version does not provide VehicleLog cloud sync.

FAQ

Where is my data stored?

On your device. Vehicles, maintenance records, documents, attachments, reminders, history, Premium entitlement, and related app data are stored locally in VehicleLog Organizer’s on-device storage.

Do I need an account?

No. VehicleLog Organizer does not use accounts or login.

Does VehicleLog Organizer use cloud sync?

No. The current version does not automatically sync your vehicle data to a VehicleLog cloud service or between devices.

How do backups work?

In the current version, Backup, Export, and Restore are Premium features. When unlocked, you start the action in the app, then use local and/or system share or file-picker flows to save, share, or choose a file. VehicleLog Organizer does not automatically upload backups to a VehicleLog server.

Keep copies of any backup or export files you create in a place you control and can access later, because those files are outside the app once shared or saved.

How do reminders work?

Reminders are created in the app and scheduled as local notifications on your device. Reminder content is not sent through a VehicleLog cloud notification service.

How do attachments work?

You can attach files or photos, and where supported you can capture photos with the device camera. Attachments are stored locally on your device and are not uploaded to a VehicleLog server. Camera, photo-library, or file access may require operating-system permission.

How do I unlock Premium?

Premium is a one-time unlock purchased through the Apple App Store or Google Play (product ID: vehiclelog_premium_unlock). Payment is handled by Apple or Google. VehicleLog Organizer does not collect your card details.

How do I restore purchases?

Use the Restore Purchases option in VehicleLog Organizer (Settings → VehicleLog Premium). The app asks Apple or Google to restore your previous purchase and then unlocks Premium locally on that device when the store confirms it. Use the same Apple ID or Google account that was used for the original purchase. Purchases are tied to the store platform where they were made and do not transfer between the Apple App Store and Google Play.

How do I delete my data?

You can:

  • Delete records or attachments inside VehicleLog Organizer, where available
  • Delete any backup or export files you saved or shared outside the app
  • Uninstall VehicleLog Organizer, which removes the app and its on-device app data from that device

Uninstalling VehicleLog Organizer does not remove copies that already exist outside the app, such as shared backups, exported files, or OS/device backups. Delete those copies separately where they were saved.

Because VehicleLog Organizer does not host your content on a VehicleLog server, there is no VehicleLog cloud account to delete.

What VehicleLog Organizer does not do (current version)

  • No VehicleLog account or login
  • No VehicleLog cloud sync
  • No VehicleLog analytics or tracking SDKs
  • No advertising
  • No AI, OCR, or image recognition
